Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of tools can be listed?

We accept design tools, resources, assets, plugins, and SaaS products that are genuinely useful for designers. All submissions go through a quality review.

Is the listing permanent?

Yes. Once your tool is approved, it stays in the Design Wallet catalog permanently. There are no monthly fees or renewals.

What if my tool is not approved?

If your tool does not pass our review, we will issue a full refund within 7 business days. We only reject tools that are not relevant to design or do not meet quality standards.

How long does it take to get listed?

Premium listings are reviewed and published within 24 hours. Free submissions may take 5-7 days. Spotlight listings are prioritised within 12 hours.

Can I update my listing later?

Premium users can request updates via email. Spotlight users get unlimited updates. Free listings cannot be modified after approval.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes. If your tool is rejected during review, you receive a full refund. Once published, refunds are not available as the listing is live.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and wallets through our payment gateway. International payments are supported as well.

Can I upgrade from Free to Premium?

Absolutely. If your tool is already listed for free, you can upgrade to Premium or Spotlight at any time by paying the difference.

Do you offer discounts for multiple tools?

Yes, we offer bundle pricing for teams listing 3+ tools. Contact us at hello@designwallet.in for a custom quote.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

There is no trial, but you can start with the Free plan to see how the catalog works before upgrading to a paid listing.
